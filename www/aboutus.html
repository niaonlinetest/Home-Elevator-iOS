About Us

Symmetry Elevator : form meets function

Beautifully Crafted, Expertly Engineered, Thoughtfully Constructed

If necessity is the mother of all invention, there is no question that a Symmetry Elevator is her child. 

The product requirements would be few, but non-negotiable.  A Symmetry product must be beautifully crafted.  It must be expertly engineered.  It must be thoughtfully constructed.  And it must be affordable. 

Our team set to its task and boldly approached the industry’s finest engineers, largest distributors, and most talented craftsmen.  The finished product beautifully embodied the original vision - superior craftsmanship and engineering at an affordable price.  The industry responded in kind, ordering Symmetry Elevators in staggering numbers, today it is the preferred residential elevator of designers, architect, fine home builders and discerning home owners all over the world.

OFFERING THE THREE LEADING ELEVATOR DRIVE SYSTEMS:

  • HYDRAULIC DRIVE SYSTEM
  • WINDING DRUM DRIVE SYSTEM
  • INLINE GEARED DRIVE SYSTEM

Symmetry has since continued its quest to develop innovative accessibility products, rigorous in its commitment to beautiful craftsmanship, expert engineering, thoughtful construction, and affordability. 

Together, Symmetry and our clients both discerning home owners, contractors and architects alike, share a singular plumb line:  excellence.An unwavering, relentless, honest pursuit of excellence.The result?  A visionary product expertly installed, unrivaled in the residential elevator industry.
